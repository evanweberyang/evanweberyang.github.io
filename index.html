Academically, I am commited to my education and excel in my studies with a CGPA of 3.96. I enjoy taking courses such as Computer Science, Software Systems, and Computer Systems, though I also spend time outside of class to attend clubs and events such as hackathons.
In addition to school, I constantly seek opportunities to expand my knowledge. I developed a Discord utility bot to enhance user experience. The bot integrates Reddit and a virtual economy into Discord, include capabilities such as sending popular posts and automating a game of blackjack. This was a bot that I started working on in early 2021 and have been continuing to maintain ever since. I used Python to develop the bot and picked up how to use MongoDB to store data. I faced many challenges in developing this bot, including various bugs, user requests, memory usage, and security vulnerabilities. The bot is currently being used by over 8000 unique Discord users and continues to grow.
I have attended various hackathons and contests and achieved various awards in web development, application development, game development, and more.
I also frequently contribute to open-source. One project I have recently contributed to is osu!, a free-to-play rhythm game.
Outside of software developing, I love playing table tennis, playing the piano, and learning languages. I find it comforting to have hobbies outside of computer science, and it helps me focus on the bigger picture.
